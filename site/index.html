Built for privacy

Your reminders and saved places stay on your device. NearNote does not require an account, does not show advertising, and does not build a location history.

Battery-efficient by design

NearNote uses Apple’s region monitoring for arrivals. Precise active location is used only while the app is open for nearby sorting, maps, and place selection.

Simple when you arrive

Actionable local notifications let you complete, snooze, or navigate to a reminder without turning location reminders into another task-management system.
